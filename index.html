Fashion

and design

FADA journey began in 2009 as a fashion boutique in Nuess, Germany. From there, we are specialized in womenswear and accessories with our crisp, chic, contemporary collections. That fashion boutique is now joined by nearly 20 agencies offering the complete FADA collection around the world.

We are proud of our Germany roots, and place great importance on quality

Production

FADA production process is a global experience. We select high-quality raw materials, such as cotton grown under the Better Cotton Initiative in India.

We do not own any factories, and work with independent suppliers and factories around the world. Our on-site FADA staff works alongside local factory employees to ensure our designs are achieved under the best operating methods. On the factory floor, we begin by knitting the fabrics we use. The knitted fabric is then passed on to the dyeing station to create the unique FADA Concept. The dyed fabric is formed as the finished garment begins to take shape. The craftsmanship involved in producing the maximum number of garments out of each piece of fabric is a true art and one that we value greatly.

From our head office in Neuss, Germany, our extensive team of designers, purchasers, assistants, pattern designers, product managers, and purchase controllers bring ideas to life.
